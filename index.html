Turning Disability into Possibility.

In a world that often looks away, TRRAIN sees potential. We don't just offer training; we offer a second chance at life for underprivileged youth and persons with disabilities. Join us in bridging the gap between talent and opportunity.

The Reality

Why our work matters now more than ever

The Severity of the Issue

Over 26.8 million people in India live with a disability. Less than 36% are employed. The gap isn't just economic; it's social.

Our Theory of Change

We believe that dignity comes from independence. By identifying marginalized individuals, providing them with market-relevant retail skills, and ensuring inclusive placements, we break the cycle of poverty and exclusion.
